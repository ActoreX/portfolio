STUDENT.INFO

Awarded as best hack for students @ 16-17.05.2015, DragonHack, Ljubljana, SI

Dragon Hack Submission by Frikoti
A webpage designed by and for students of FRI faculty of Ljubljana. Our intent was to gather all relevant educational info on a single page from: E-classroom, STUDIS exam data system, timetables and Relevant Facebook feed

eZdravko - health assistant

School project - OIS HW 4 @ 21. 12. 2014

Web application eZdravko is intended for people, who want to track thier health information, stats and some other activities (measurement of height, weight, temperature, blood pressure, blood oxygen saturation, etc.). In addtion, platform offers a preview of all prescribed medicines(in past), their potential side effects and its alternatives - similar products. In some cases eZdravko should detect specific abnormalities about patient's health and immediately imformed the patient about that and also gave him an advice(in some extent) if possible(eg. what to do in case of malnutrition, abnormal blood pressure, etc ...) - in other words, eZdravko could be the patient best friend and sometimes even save lives.
